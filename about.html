About Us

Hi, Janice and Amy here

We are mother and daughter and are here to provide solutions for whatever property needs you have in Spain.

Both from administration and customer service backgrounds, we have the necessary skills to organise whatever it is that you require. More importantly, our personal values of honesty and integrity means that you are able to put your complete trust in us. We understand that it can be difficult and, at times, stressful owning a property so far away from your main one, and we always have your interests at the heart of everything we do.

Because we deal with every aspect of owning a property in Spain, we are different to most agents. We believe that our job does not begin and end with a sale. We want to be part of your whole journey, from the amazingly exciting time when you find the holiday home of your dreams and your offer is accepted, then being here to provide whatever service you need once your own your dream home, then finally helping you to sell and move on when the time is right.

Please feel free to contact us for a no-obligation chat if you would like to know more about any of our services and to find out about our fees.
