Holiday Rental Service

We offer a rental service that is more comprehensive than any other agent and deal with absolutely everything for you. We advertise your property and deal with all enquiries and reservations; we look after your guests during their stay and carry out a departure inspection after they have left. We deal with taking of all payments, keeping detailed records of income and expenditure, and then provide the relevant information to an accountant in Spain to ensure you are legally compliant with the tax regulations. We also deal with everything you need to do, to be compliant with the holiday rental laws.

Long Term Rental Service

If you are no longer visiting your property often, but want to maintain it as an investment, then you could consider renting it on a long term basis. We have a thorough understanding of the laws governing renting long term in Spain, so that you can make an informed decision on whether this option is right for you. Again, our service is comprehensive and in addition to dealing with the management of your property, we also collect the rent and utility payments and keep a detailed account of income and expenditure for you. We can also assist by providing this information to an accountant in Spain.
