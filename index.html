Your Spanish Property Solutions

We are part of the Murcia Villas Group, who have been providing estate agency, property management and rental services in the region for over 20 years. When you entrust us to look after your property, you can rest assured that you are in safe and professional hands.

Whatever assistance you require, we can provide it. Here is a brief overview of the services that we offer.

Management Service

This is our most popular service. We will hold your keys and be your point of contact in Spain. Once a month we will visit your property and undertake a thorough inspection, following which you will receive a condition report by email. At your request we can visit more frequently, at an additional cost. If you rent out your property yourself, we can organise the cleaning and laundry services you will need.

Rental Service

If you want to rent your property commercially, then this is the service for you. We have a wealth of experience in this area and can guide you around all you need to do to comply with any rental and tax legislation. We can provide a service where we manage your property and the rentals, or we are also happy to work with your existing property managers and manage only the rentals.

Key Holding Service

This is our most basic service, where we will hold your keys and be your point of contact in Spain. This service is suitable for owners who visit their property frequently and only require us to be available for ad hoc visits. Within this service we will organise cleaning and laundry for any of your own visits. If you require us to organise the cleaning and laundry for multiple visits by friends and family, we can do this at an additional cost.
