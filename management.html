Management or Key Holding Services

You have your holiday home, so what next...?

Whether you are new to property ownership in Spain, or have owned for many years, you will want to entrust the care of your holiday home to professionals. We provide services of different levels, to suit whatever needs you have.

Key Holding Service

This is our basic service, for properties that are not let out and where owners do not require any cleaning and laundry services. It covers the cost of holding your keys and being your point of contact in Spain for any issues that might arise with your property, which we would deal with on your behalf. If you need us to visit your property for any reason, we charge an hourly rate with a minimum of one hour as a call-out charge.

Property Management Service

This service is for when you require regular visits to your holiday home, properties that are rented out, or where you want us to arrange a cleaning and laundry service for your visits. Your property will be visited monthly and a condition report sent to you afterwards. Time spent arranging any remedial work required is charged at an hourly rate, but your consent would be obtained prior to any work being undertaken, except in emergencies.
